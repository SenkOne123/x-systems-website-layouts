Market alerts

Alert

Market alert is the result of technical analysis based on statistical and historical data.

Alerts show information about the current price forex or crypto asset at the time of appearance of the alert and the recommended price values for Stop Loss and Take Profit levels, at which a trader can open a buy or sell deal.

The Power and Heatmap shows the profitability of past market alerts across various technical analysis indicators, given similar market performance at the moment.

The trading panel is designed for automatic trading. Having chosen the parameters for placing stop orders, the trader can press the quick button with the desired lot size and the deal will automatically open.

Dashboard

Support & Resistance

The basis of this technical instrument is a pivot point and additional lines that act as resistance and support levels. The location of these lines directly depends on the position of the extreme points of the previous session and the opening and closing prices. A trader can use this knowledge to build a trading strategy.

Buyers and sellers in the market

The Summary panel and the Bulls & Bears indicator help the trader assess the current balance of buyers and sellers in the market. If the buyers are stronger than the sellers for a period of time, the market is in an uptrend. Accordingly, if sellers are stronger, the price of a particular asset decreases until a balance is established.

Stochastic oscillators

CCI shows the deviation of the price of an asset from its average price over a certain period of time. RSI shows the moments when an asset may be in a state of overbought or oversold. Traders use indicators to find market entry and exit points.

Trends

Trends

The Trend indicator allows you to determine the strength and direction of the current trend. With the help of the indicator, a trader can see what trend is currently developing in the market.

Volatility

The Volatility indicator clearly shows the trader the amplitude of market movements within a certain time period. This helps to assess the current state of the market, as well as to predict in which direction the price will go in the near future.
