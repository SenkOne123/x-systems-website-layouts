What is forex trading

Forex is the largest and most active financial market in the world, where transactions for the sale and purchase of various currencies are conducted with the goal of making a profit from the change in their rates.

Forex is also the most liquid financial market in the world. This means that on forex you can quickly and easily convert any asset into cash at a price that is close to market price.

The main feature of the forex market is that currency is both a commodity and a means of payment. The price of one currency is linked to the price of another currency. Therefore, trades are always made with currency pairs that consist of a base currency and a quote currency. The difference between the sale and purchase price is called the spread. The measure of the change in price difference of a currency pair is known as pip (percentage in point).

A forex trader’s profit or loss depends on the change in the difference in the price of currencies. Currency prices in the forex market are often indicated with an accuracy to five decimal places because their spread is usually very small. And even slight price variations in several pips can result in a significant profit since the amounts of transactions in the exchange market are often in the millions. However, large trading volumes mean that even a small spread can lead to large losses.

In order to trade on forex today, you only need internet access and a special software application that is available to anyone.

The forex market is open 24 hours a day, 5 days a week. Working hours are divided into trading sessions based on geographic area: Pacific, European, Asian, and American. Although it is possible to trade on forex any time, professional traders choose trading times based on their own trading strategies.

Forex trading in the US

Is forex trading legal in US

Forex trading is a completely legal way to make money. Although stock trading is more popular in the US, many US traders choose forex to start trading because it requires less initial money than stocks.

The activity of companies that offer services related to trading forex in the US are regulated by the NFA (National Futures Association) and the CFTC (Commodity Futures Trading Commission). All brokers in the US must be members of the NFA, and the CFTC is responsible for adherence to strict regulations. Furthermore, brokers require much more capital in the US than in any other country.

In connection with this, as a rule many European or Asian brokers do not offer their services for American traders. However, the strict rules and laws of the US have made forex trading safer, more stable, and less risky than in other countries.

Taxes on forex trading
in the US

Forex trading rules in the US differ from rules in many other countries around the world. And as usual, they are much stricter. US citizens must declare everything they make from foreign exchange trading in the US and any world foreign exchange markets. They also must pay taxes on their profits anywhere in the world. This law requires that all foreign brokers provide information about their accounts of US citizens to the Internal Revenue Service (IRS).
