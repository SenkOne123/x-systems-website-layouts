Why do you need a broker?

A broker is an important participant in the process of trading on the Forex international currency market. The broker provides the trader with a convenient trading platform. But in order to trade Forex, you need to be a licensed member. Brokers have the necessary licenses so that clients, even with small start-up capital, can trade and make a profit.

How to choose a broker?

When choosing a broker, you need to make sure that the broker you choose has the assets that you want to trade. If you are a beginner, choose a broker that will allow you to make a minimum deposit. Explore the types and sizes of brokerage commissions. The longer a broker has been in the market, the more reliable it is. Choose a broker with responsive, professional, and experienced customer service representatives. An important factor is also the honesty and openness of information about the conditions for working with a broker.

Why pay a commission?

The commission is the funds that the broker takes for the maintenance of the trading account and trader's transactions. Basically, write-offs are calculated as a percentage of the turnover of funds. A good broker works under transparent and understandable conditions, information on the cost of his services should be indicated on the website. When making a deposit, newbies should study all the conditions of working with a broker and make sure that there are no hidden fees that can take most of the deposit.

What is the broker's commission?

Each broker sets the size of the commission individually. The main types of commissions:

  • - Spread. This is the main fee that the brokerage company charges for transactions. The difference between the buy and sell price of an instrument.
  • - Swap. Leverage fee. Usually debited daily, in case of opening deals.
  • - Commission for opening deals. Forex brokers' commission usually does not exceed $ 5-10 per 1 lot.
  • - Payments for deposits and withdrawals. Usually no more than $ 1 of the amount
What is a broker rating?

To protect yourself from unreliable brokers, you need to carefully study and analyze the rating of Forex brokers. The rating of brokers in the foreign exchange market is an ordered list of companies, sorted by the assessment of the quality of service provision. In the first place are the leaders of the Forex market, who have repeatedly proved their reliability and won the largest number of positive ratings from clients. The last places are occupied by outsiders or beginners, who, due to their short period of existence, do not yet have a sufficient number of positive responses from traders.
